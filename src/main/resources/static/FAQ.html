  • New to The Fellowship?

  • About

    The Fellowship aims to provide a social platform where updated information regarding international activites or opportunities between the Munich University of Applied Science (MUAS) and Cal Poly communities may be shared. It is an interactive collection of information for the use of students, professors, grant organizations, and all others of the university community.

  • How do I sign up?

    Account holders have the privelage of sharing their experiences via posting, and are also able to like and comment on other posts as well. To register, you will need to provide your name, MUAS or Cal Poly associated email and a password of your choice. To finish creating your account, you email via a code or link. Thereafter, you may login to The Fellowship with your email and chosen password.

  • Posts

  • How do I create a post?

    To create a post, click within the "create new post" text field to share your experience with others! You can add attachments such as picures or videos as well as tags to categorize your post. When finished, simply click "Submit" and your post should appear on the Newsfeed!

  • How do I comment on or like a specific post?

    While browsing the Newsfeed, you might find a post that you love and would like to express your thoughts to the author. Simply click the "like" button to like the post or click "comment" to add text just as you would when creating a post.

  • Newsfeed

  • How do I search for a specific post?

    Start by entering keywords or tags in the Search bar located on the left side of the website. If posts with the appropriate tags exist, they will be shown on the Newsfeed.

  • How do I filter posts on the Newsfeed?

    If you would like to only view posts on the Newsfeed that fall under a certain category, simply select the desired filter under "Filters" on the left sidebar of the web page. The Newsfeed will adjust accordingly to ensure that we show you what you want to see!

  • What is the "Stat" button?

    Our stat button is meant to provide additional information about the collection of different types of posts on The Fellowship (i.e. number of total posts in Software Engineering or number of total posts about research opportunities). Administrators may use this data for analytical purposes with a goal to create new or modify existing international programs to better cater to the university community's needs.

  • Your Account

  • Login to The Fellowship

    If you already have an existing account with The Fellowship, follow the login button at the top right corner of the main page and provide your university affiliated email. If you do not have an account with The Fellowship, please refer above to "How do I sign up?"

  • Logout of The Fellowship

    To logout, select "Sign out" in the drop down menu of the "Profile" option at the top of the page. Once signed out, you will no longer be able to view or create posts and like or comment on other posts. Note: you will still be able to view all posts on our Newsfeed as well as use the filtering option.
